Coquito

Coquito is a creamy, tropical, Puerto Rican holiday drink with tons of coconut flavor. It's made with both evaporated and condensed milk, cream of coconut, spices, rum, and — in this version — egg yolks.

It is always requested at my holiday gatherings. Sometimes I hide it in the fridge to keep for myself! Serve in glass cups and sprinkle with more cinnamon if desired. Feliz Navidad!

Total Time: 15 mins

Servings: 10

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of evaporated Milk.
  • 2 beaten Egg yolks
  • 1 can of Cream of Coconut
  • Sweetened condensed Milk
  • White Rum
  • Water
  • 1 teaspoon of Vanilla
  • Spices. We recommend cinnamon.

    • How to Make:

    1. Cook the evaporated milk and egg yolks in a double boiler until thick.
    2. Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend with the remaining ingredients.
    3. Transfer to glass bottles and refrigerator until cold.
