Baked Brie

This Brie cheese recipe wraps a whole wheel of Brie cheese filled with preserves in puff pastry.

It's baked until the pastry is golden and crisp and Brie is warm and gooey inside for a cheesy treat.

Total Time: 40 mins

Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 8-Ounce wheel of Brie Cheese
  • Preserves. We recommend peach or apricot.
  • 1 sheet of Puff pastry
  • 1 Egg white

    • How to Make:

    1. Slice Brie cheese wheel in half so that you have two circles of cheese.
    2. Sandwich the preserves between the cheese halves, then reassemble.
    3. Wrap the entire wheel of Brie with the puff pastry.
    4. Transfer onto the prepared baking sheet seam-side down.
    5. Brush puff pastry with egg white.
